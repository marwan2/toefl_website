TOEFL® Paper-delivered Testing

In October 2017, we launched the revised TOEFL® Paper-delivered Test, a new paper-and-pencil test that is more closely aligned to the TOEFL iBT® test.

The TOEFL® PBT test is no longer administered, but your scores are valid for 2 years after your test date. For more information, including how to order additional score reports, see TOEFL® PBT test.
